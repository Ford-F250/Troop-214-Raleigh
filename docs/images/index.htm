Welcome to the Troop 214 Website

Troop 214 is a proud part of the Boy Scouts of America's Occonneechee Council, Falls District, and the Kato Hochuli (Lodge 104) Order of the Arrow. Troop 214 is chartered by (and is grateful to) Benson Memorial United Methodist Church in Raleigh, NC.

This website is maintained by the Scouts of Troop 214, under the supervision of the Troop 214 Website Committee. All content is approved by the Troop Committee.
